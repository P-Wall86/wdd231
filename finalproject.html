WDD 231 - INDIVIDUAL PROJECT SITE PLAN

Site Name

The site name is "Pawsome Homes". The name blends "paws" and "awesome," celebrating the love of adopted pets and the families who welcome them. A name as heartwarming and joyful as the bonds it creates. Optional domain availability: pawsomehomes.com.ar

Site Purpose

Paws & Home is a community-driven platform dedicated to matching cats and dogs of all ages with loving forever families. We showcase adoptable pets, support local adoption programs, and connect pet owners with essential veterinary care—because every animal deserves safety, health, and happiness. By promoting education and community engagement, we tackle pet abandonment and improve animal welfare, one adoption at a time.

Scenarios

"How can a busy family with no pet experience find the perfect first dog—and ensure a smooth transition for both pet and household?"

"How can an overwhelmed animal rescuer quickly place 10 stray kittens into responsible homes—while also recruiting long-term fosters?"

Color Schema

This palette creates a warm and accessible design. Dark Slate Gray (#2F4F4F) is used for headings and the header/footer, adding stability. Floral White (#FDF6EC) and Sandy Brown (#F4A261) keeps the background inviting, while Jet (#333333) ensures easy-to-read text. Gamboge (#E8A22C) highlights CTAs, drawing attention effectively. The colors work together to enhance clarity, warmth, and engagement.

Typography

Poppins is used for headings and subtitles. Lora is used for body text.
